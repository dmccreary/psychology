# Chapter 16: Treatment and Therapy

## Summary

This final chapter surveys the full landscape of psychological treatment.
Students compare psychodynamic, humanistic, cognitive, behavioral, and
cognitive-behavioral therapies, then examine biological treatments —
antidepressants, antianxiety medications, antipsychotics, lithium, ECT,
and TMS. The chapter also covers biofeedback, hypnosis, and group therapy
modalities. Key disorders from Chapter 15 — PTSD, bipolar disorders,
dissociative disorders, panic disorder, and eating disorders — are revisited
through a treatment lens. The chapter closes with stigma reduction, culturally
responsive care, and posttraumatic growth.
