# Chapter 15: Psychological Disorders

## Summary

This chapter provides in-depth coverage of the major disorder categories
assessed on the AP exam. Starting from the foundations in Chapter 14, students
examine neurodevelopmental disorders (ADHD, autism spectrum), the schizophrenia
spectrum (positive/negative symptoms), depressive and bipolar disorders,
anxiety disorders (agoraphobia, social anxiety, GAD, panic disorder), OCD and
hoarding disorder, personality disorder clusters (A/B/C), antisocial and
borderline personality disorders, dissociative disorders, eating disorders
(anorexia and bulimia), PTSD, and the diathesis-stress model. Sociocultural
context — including ethnocentrism in diagnosis — runs throughout.
